Payment Method

Payment Method: Gcash

Welcome to our payment page! To complete your purchase, please use Gcash following these simple steps:

  1. 1. Open the Gcash App
    • Launch the Gcash app on your mobile device and log in to your account.
  2. 2. Select "Send Money"
    • Tap on "Send Money" from the main menu.
  3. 3. Choose "Express Send"
    • Enter our Gcash number: (e.g., 0912-345-6789)
  4. 4. Enter the Amount
    • Type in the total amount for your order.
  5. 5. Review and Confirm
    • Double-check the amount and confirm the transaction.
  6. 6. Save a Screenshot
    • Take a screenshot of the payment confirmation or save your transaction ID as proof.

After Payment:

Once you have sent the payment, please wait a moment for your unique Order Number to appear below. This order number will serve as proof that your payment was received.

Important Information

Present this Order Number at the coop to confirm your purchase.

If you have any issues or need assistance, feel free to contact our support team at qcumerchmart@gmail.com.
