Help Center

Welcome to our Help Center! We're here to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have regarding your experience with MerchMart. Below, you’ll find answers to some common inquiries. If you need further assistance, don’t hesitate to reach out to us.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  1. 1. How do I place an order?
    • Simply browse our selection of school supplies, add your desired items to the cart, and proceed to checkout. Once you complete your order, you will receive an Order Number for pickup.
  2. 2. What payment methods do you accept?
    • We currently accept Gcash for online payments. Additionally, you can choose the On-Hand Payment Method, where you pay for your order upon pickup at our location.
  3. 3. How do I pick up my order?
    • After you've placed your order and received your Order Number, visit us at our location. Just present your Order Number, and our staff will have your items ready for you.
  4. 4. Can I change or cancel my order?
    • If you need to change or cancel your order, please contact us as soon as possible. We’ll do our best to accommodate your request before your order is processed.
  5. 5. What should I do if I have a problem with my order?
    • If you encounter any issues with your order, please contact our support team at merchmart@gmail.com. We’re here to help resolve any problems.

Contact Us

If you have any issues or need assistance, feel free to contact our support team at qcumerchmart@gmail.com.

Thank you for your order, and we look forward to serving you!
